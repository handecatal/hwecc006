About Us

Blossom Basket has been around in the flower shop industry since year 2018 and we still continue to provide quality and surpassing service for our valued customers in Cyprus. The founder Hande Catal has more than a decade of experience in the flower arrangement industry which serves as one of the strength of Blossom Basket as a company. Our mission is about meeting customers need and to provide unique-personalized flower arrangements for any occasion that can help customers enhance relationships with the important people in their lives. And to offer quality floral products at affordable prices so our customers receive the best value for their money.
