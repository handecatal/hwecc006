Wedding

The Blossom Basket aim to bring you something beautiful, stylish and unique, something a little bit different for your wedding day. We specialise in providing stunning flowers for weddings, from a single buttonhole through to elaborate church or venue arrangements, using a personal and tailor made approach that ensures each bride gets exactly what they want and more.

Our Services

  Consultation: Your wedding florist will meet with you to discuss your vision for your wedding—your venue, your wedding style, your desired color palette, and the types of flowers you like and dislike. Your florist will then create a proposal which will describe the arrangements he or she will create as well as the prices.
  Samples: After you've signed a contract and shortly before your wedding, your florist may also provide sample arrangements to ensure that you're on the same page about how your wedding-day flowers will look.
  Flowers: A big part of your wedding flower budget will go to the actual purchasing of the flowers. It actually takes several hours for a florist to put a flower order together—and the flowers your florist will receive from his or her wholesaler or grower are far better quality than the ones you can buy at your local grocery store.
